Frequently Asked Questions

Are you affiliated with any utility company?

No. MyEnergyProvider is an independent third-party bill payment service. We are NOT affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to any utility company including Duke Energy, PG&E, SCE, FPL, or any other provider mentioned on our website.

Do you charge a fee for your service?

Yes. We charge a convenience fee for our phone-based payment service. The exact fee amount will be clearly disclosed to you before your payment is processed. You have the right to decline our service at any time.

Can I pay my bill for free elsewhere?

Yes. You can pay your utility bill directly through your utility provider's official website, mobile app, or customer service line, typically at no additional cost. Our service is an optional convenience for those who prefer phone-based assistance.

How does your service work?

Call (888) 524-0250, provide your utility provider and account information, and our representative will help process your payment. Before processing, we will disclose our service fee. You can decline at any time.

Is my payment information secure?

We use encrypted payment processing. Your payment details are transmitted securely and we do not store your complete card information after the transaction.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), debit cards, and bank transfers (ACH).

What are your hours of operation?

Our phone service is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
