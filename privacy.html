Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Note: MyEnergyProvider is a third-party bill payment service. We are NOT a utility company and are NOT affiliated with any utility provider.

Information We Collect

When you use our payment service, we collect: your name, phone number, email address, utility account information, and payment details necessary to process your transaction.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to: process your bill payment, provide customer support, send payment confirmations, and comply with legal requirements. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Security

Payment information is encrypted during transmission. We do not store complete payment card numbers after transactions are processed. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information.

Information Sharing

We share information only with payment processors necessary to complete transactions, and as required by law. We do not share your information with utility companies (we are not affiliated with them).
