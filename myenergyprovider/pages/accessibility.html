Accessibility Statement

Our commitment to making energy bill payments accessible to everyone

Our Commitment

MyEnergyProvider is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards to guarantee we provide equal access to all users.

Accessibility by Design

Our phone-based bill payment service is designed to be inherently accessible. By providing voice-guided payment processing, we ensure that customers who may have difficulty using online interfaces can easily manage their energy bill payments.

Conformance Status

We strive to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with disabilities and more user-friendly for everyone.

Accessibility Features

Our website and services include the following accessibility features:

Keyboard Navigation

All functionality is accessible via keyboard for users who cannot use a mouse.

Screen Reader Compatible

Our website is designed to work with popular screen readers including JAWS, NVDA, and VoiceOver.

Alternative Text

All images include descriptive alternative text for users of assistive technologies.

Color Contrast

We maintain sufficient color contrast ratios for text readability.

Resizable Text

Text can be resized up to 200% without loss of content or functionality.

Phone Service

Our phone-based payment system provides an alternative to web interfaces.

Phone-Based Accessibility

Our primary service is phone-based bill payment, which offers inherent accessibility advantages:

  • Voice-Guided Assistance: Live representatives guide you through the payment process
  • No Visual Interface Required: Complete your payment entirely by voice
  • Clear Audio Communication: Our phone system is optimized for clarity
  • Patient Support: Our representatives take the time needed to assist every customer
  • TTY/TDD Support: We provide text telephone services for deaf or hard-of-hearing customers
  • Language Assistance: Multilingual support is available upon request

Technical Specifications

Accessibility of our website relies on the following technologies:

  • HTML5
  • WAI-ARIA (Web Accessibility Initiative - Accessible Rich Internet Applications)
  • CSS (Cascading Style Sheets)
  • JavaScript

These technologies are relied upon for conformance with the accessibility standards used.

Known Limitations

While we strive to ensure accessibility of our website and services, there may be some limitations. Below is a description of known limitations:

  • Third-Party Content: Some content from third parties may not be fully accessible
  • PDF Documents: Older PDF documents may not be fully accessible; we are working to remediate these
  • Maps: Interactive maps may pose challenges for screen reader users

We are actively working to address these issues and improve accessibility across all our content.

Feedback and Assistance

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of MyEnergyProvider. If you encounter accessibility barriers on our website or need assistance with our services, please contact us:

  • Phone: (888) 524-0250
  • Email: [email protected]
  • TTY/TDD: 711 (Telecommunications Relay Service)

Response Time

We aim to respond to accessibility feedback within 2 business days. If you need immediate assistance with making a payment, please call our main line at (888) 524-0250 where our representatives can provide personalized support.

Compatibility with Browsers and Assistive Technology

Our website is designed to be compatible with the following assistive technologies:

  • Screen readers (JAWS, NVDA, VoiceOver, TalkBack)
  • Screen magnification software
  • Speech recognition software
  • Keyboard-only navigation

The website is designed to be compatible with recent versions of major browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.

Assessment and Remediation

MyEnergyProvider assesses the accessibility of our website through:

  • Self-evaluation using accessibility testing tools
  • Regular review of user feedback
  • Testing with various assistive technologies
  • Periodic external accessibility audits

Updates to This Statement

This accessibility statement was last updated in January 2025. We review and update this statement regularly as we continue to improve accessibility across our services.
