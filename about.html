About MyEnergyProvider

Making energy bill payments simple, secure, and stress-free since 2018.

Our Mission

At MyEnergyProvider, we believe that paying your energy bill shouldn't be a complicated process. Our mission is to simplify bill payments for millions of Americans by providing a friendly, phone-based payment service that anyone can use.

Whether you're a senior citizen who prefers speaking to a real person, a busy professional who doesn't have time for complex online systems, or simply someone who values convenience, we're here to help.

  • Serving customers across all 50 states
  • Supporting 15+ major energy providers
  • Available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week
  • PCI-compliant secure payment processing

Our Story

Founded in 2018, MyEnergyProvider was born from a simple observation: millions of Americans struggle with paying their utility bills online. Complex websites, confusing interfaces, and long hold times with utility companies create unnecessary stress.

Our founders saw an opportunity to create a better way. By building a dedicated team of payment specialists and partnering with major energy providers across the country, we created a streamlined phone-based payment service that makes bill paying as easy as making a phone call.

Today, we've helped over 50,000 customers pay their energy bills quickly and securely, and we're just getting started.
