Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Information We Collect

When you use our phone-based bill payment service, we may collect the following information:

  • Your name and contact information
  • Your energy provider account number
  • Payment information (credit card, debit card, or bank account details)
  • Call recordings for quality assurance purposes

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process your energy bill payments
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our services
  • Comply with legal requirements

Information Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. All payment data is encrypted using SSL/TLS technology, and we are PCI-DSS compliant. We do not store complete credit card numbers after transactions are processed.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties except as necessary to process your payments or comply with legal requirements. We may share information with payment processors and energy providers solely for the purpose of completing your transactions.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@myenergyprovider.com or call (888) 525-0250.
