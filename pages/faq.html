Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our energy bill payment service.

How does your service work?

Simply call our toll-free number (888) 525-0250 and speak with a friendly representative. Provide your energy provider, account number, and payment information. We'll process your payment instantly and provide confirmation.

Which energy providers do you support?

We support all major US energy providers including Duke Energy, Southern Company, PG&E, Con Edison, Dominion Energy, Exelon, NextEra Energy, AEP, Xcel Energy, Entergy, and many more. If you don't see your provider listed, call us ‚Äì we likely support them!

Is my payment information secure?

Yes! We use bank-level encryption to protect all payment information. Our systems are PCI-DSS compliant, and we never store your complete credit card number after the transaction is processed.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), debit cards, and ACH bank transfers from checking or savings accounts.

Is there a fee for using your service?

There may be a small convenience fee for processing payments. Our representatives will inform you of any applicable fees before processing your payment, so there are no surprises.

How quickly is my payment processed?

Most payments are processed instantly. You'll receive confirmation before you hang up. The payment typically reflects on your energy account within 1-2 business days.

Are you affiliated with my energy provider?

No. MyEnergyProvider.com is an independent third-party bill payment service. We are NOT affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any energy provider. We simply provide a convenient way to pay your bills over the phone.

What hours are you available?

Our representatives are available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. You can pay your bill anytime, including holidays.
